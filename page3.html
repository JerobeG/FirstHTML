How Can We Minimize the Impact of Computer Production?

  1. Produce products that use less amount of electricity
    • Intel and AMD announced that instead of creating products that are faster than their predecessors, they will focus on creating products that are the same speed but use less electricity
    • Using less electricity will reduce the amount of greenhouse gasses used
  2. Green Manufacturing
  • Green manufacturing the process of using less natural resources to create a product
  • Computers require natural resources like lead and mercury, which can only be obtained from mining from produces greenhouse gasses
  • Reducing these resources where the only way of obtaining them cause greenhouse gasses will obviously reduce greenhouse gasses
